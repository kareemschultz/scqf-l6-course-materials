ASSESSMENT #2 Group Project - The Recruitment and Selection Process
Assignment
Opened: Friday, 20 March 2026, 9:00 AM
Due: Wednesday, 25 March 2026, 11:59 PM

This is a Group Project -20%. Students will work in groups. Each group must answer all questions in a Power Point Document on the LE by ... April 25th by 11.59pm.

Q#1 Name 5 Activities in correct order that the HR Manager expects to be done to complete the The Recruitment Segment of the Process?...4 mks

Q#2- Name 4 Distinct  Methods of External Recruitment ...2mks

Q#3 - Name TWO Advantages of External Recruitment - Explain both in

 ONE sentence each....2 mks

Q#4 - Name Two Disadvantages of Internal Recruitment and Explain each in ONE sentence each....2 mks.

Q#5 - Why is it important to complete the steps in the Recruitment stage of the process before conducting the Selection Stage of the Process?...2 Marks

Q#6 - Explain How Bio Data Inventory and Background Investigations can be used by Human Resource Managers to assist them in selecting the right person for the Job Position....4 Marks.

Q#7- In the Selection process, when Interviewing Candidates - Explain how these These TWO types of Information assist in making the Choice/Decision. Candidate Self Evaluation and  *Behavior Description Information? 2 Marks.

Q#8 - Interview Content and Question Types - How can STRESS be assessed or measured in A Candidate during2 "One to One" or "Face to Face" Interview e.g HR Manager and Candidate....2 Marks

Total 20 Marks

THE END

 Each student in the group must complete their agreed segment to the PowerPoint Presentation in the Group Project - 20% 

The Group Leader MUST upload to the Learning Exchange by the specified time.

The Mark earned by the Group goes to ALL, therefore it is important that there is consensus of the full document before submission....2 MARKS.

NO citations nor Referencing required.
