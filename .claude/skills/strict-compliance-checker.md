# WRITING STYLE REQUIREMENTS

## To Avoid AI Detection:

### AVOID These AI Red Flags:
- "Furthermore", "Moreover", "Additionally" (overused)
- "It is important to note that..."
- "In today's modern world..."
- "This essay will explore..."
- Perfect, identical paragraph lengths
- Overly formal, robotic language
- "Utilize" instead of "use"

### USE Natural Language:
- Vary sentence length
- Use contractions occasionally ("don't", "it's")
- Include personal observations ("I noticed...", "In my view...")
- Show thinking process ("At first I thought X, but...")
- Use specific examples with real companies
- Read aloud - if it sounds robotic, rewrite

### Good Sentence Starters:
- "Looking at..."
- "One thing that stands out is..."
- "What's interesting here is..."
- "This shows that..."
- "For example..."
- "A good illustration of this is..."
