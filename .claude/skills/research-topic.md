# Research Topic for Assignment

Help research and gather information for SCQF Level 6 business assignments

## Instructions

You help SCQF Level 6 students research topics for their assignments. Guide them in finding reliable sources and understanding key concepts.

### Research Process

1. **Define the Research Question**
   - What exactly does the assignment ask?
   - What learning outcomes must be addressed?
   - What specific information do you need?

2. **Identify Key Concepts**
   - List the main topics to research
   - Identify relevant theories/frameworks
   - Note any specific requirements (e.g., real company examples)

3. **Find Reliable Sources**
   - Academic sources (journals, textbooks)
   - Business publications
   - Company websites and reports
   - News articles (recent)
   - Government/official statistics

4. **Evaluate Sources**
   - Is it from a credible author/organisation?
   - Is it recent enough?
   - Is it relevant to your question?
   - Can you reference it properly?

5. **Organize Findings**
   - Group by topic/theme
   - Note key quotes and page numbers
   - Record full citation details

## Recording Research

### Create a Research Log

For each source, record:
```
Source Type: [Book/Journal/Website/Report]
Author(s):
Title:
Year:
Publisher/Website:
URL (if online):
Access Date:

Key Information Found:
- Point 1
- Point 2
- Point 3

Relevant Quote: "[exact quote]" (p. X)

How I'll Use This:
```

### Organizing Notes

Group your notes by:
1. **Learning Outcome** - Which criteria does this address?
2. **Theme/Topic** - What subject area?
3. **Theory vs Example** - Is this a concept or a case study?

## Avoiding Common Research Mistakes

1. **Don't rely on Wikipedia** - Use it to find real sources, not as a source itself
2. **Check dates** - Business information gets outdated quickly
3. **Verify claims** - Cross-reference important facts
4. **Note page numbers** - You'll need them for citations
5. **Don't over-research** - Focus on what you need for the assignment
6. **Keep track of sources** - Record everything as you go

## Research Questions by Assignment

### For Understanding Business:
- "How is [company] structured and why?"
- "What external factors are currently affecting [industry]?"
- "What conflicts exist between [stakeholder A] and [stakeholder B]?"

### For Management of People and Finance:
- "How does [company] recruit and train staff?"
- "How do motivation theories apply in [company/industry]?"
- "What are the main sources of finance for [company type]?"

### For Contemporary Business Issues:
- "What challenges did [SME] face during its growth phase?"
- "How did [company] adapt its strategy during [life cycle stage]?"
- "What internal and external factors affected [SME's] development?"

## Creating Your Research Plan

1. List all topics you need to cover
2. Allocate time for each topic
3. Identify 2-3 sources per topic
4. Read and note key points
5. Find real company examples
6. Organize by assignment structure
7. Start writing with your research ready
