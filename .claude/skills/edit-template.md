# Edit Assignment Template

Help fill in and edit the provided JAIN College assignment templates (docx files)

## Instructions

You help students work with the official JAIN College SCQF Level 6 assignment templates. These templates are provided as .docx files that students must use for submission.

### Available Templates

The following templates are available in this directory:

| Template File | Unit Code | Unit Title |
|--------------|-----------|------------|
| HE9E 46_Contemporary Business Issues_Student Assessment Template.docx | HE9E 46 | Contemporary Business Issues |
| J229 76_Understanding Business._Student Assessment Template.docx | J229 76 | Understanding Business |
| J22A 76_Management of People and Finance_Student_Assessment Template.docx | J22A 76 | Management of People and Finance |

### Template Structure

Each template contains:

1. **Cover Page** (Page 1)
   - Assignment Brief header
   - Unit Information (pre-filled)
   - Assignment title and task breakdown
   - Marks distribution
   - Dates
   - Student information fields (TO BE FILLED)
   - Extension checkbox
   - Marker section

2. **Guidelines Pages** (Pages 2-3)
   - General Guidelines for Assessment
   - Word Processing Guidance
   - Assignment Submission rules
   - Declaration of Authenticity
   - Statement of Originality (TO BE SIGNED)

3. **Task Pages** (Pages 4+)
   - Assignment tasks with instructions
   - Space for student responses

---

## Fields Students Must Complete

### On Cover Page:
- **Student USN:** Your university serial number
- **College Name & Site:** JAIN College
- **Tutor:** Your tutor's name
- **Date Due:** The submission deadline
- **Date of Submission:** When you actually submit

### On Declaration Page:
- **Student's Signature:** Sign (can be typed name for electronic)
- **Date:** Date of signing

### In Footer (Every Page):
Format: `Student USN_Name_Unit Code_Unit Title_Page Number`

Example: `JC2024001_Rahul Kumar_J229 76_Understanding Business_Page 5`

---

## How to Edit the Template

### Using Microsoft Word:

1. **Open the template file**
2. **Save As** with your name: `USN_Name_UnitCode_Assignment1.docx`
3. **Fill in cover page details**
4. **Complete the declaration**
5. **Set up footer:**
   - Double-click footer area
   - Type: `YourUSN_YourName_UnitCode_UnitTitle_Page `
   - Insert > Page Number > Current Position
6. **Write your assignment content** in the designated task areas
7. **Add References section** at the end
8. **Save and submit**

### Using Google Docs:

1. Upload the .docx to Google Drive
2. Open with Google Docs
3. Make your edits
4. Download as .docx for submission

## Assignment-Specific Requirements

### HE9E 46 - Contemporary Business Issues

**Task Distribution:** 50% Task 1, 50% Task 2

**Outcome 1 Requirements:**
- Define an SME (use EU/OECD definition)
- Explain at least 3 key features of SMEs
- Explain characteristics at each of 5 life cycle stages (start-up, growth, maturity, renewal, decline)
- Outline 2+ objectives for each life cycle stage

**Outcome 2 Requirements:**
- Explore 2+ internal and 2+ external challenges
- Explain role of 4+ functional activities across life cycle stages
- Analyse 2+ business strategies with expected effects

---

### J229 76 - Understanding Business

**Task Distribution:** 50% Task A, 50% Task B

**Outcome 1 Requirements:**
- Compare features of large organisations from different sectors
- Identify objectives and describe their importance
- Describe 3+ internal structures with justification

**Outcome 2 Requirements:**
- Explain 2+ internal factors' impact
- Explain 2+ external factors' impact (PESTLE factors)
- Describe 2+ stakeholder conflicts of interest

---

### J22A 76 - Management of People and Finance

**Task Distribution:** 60% Task A, 40% Task B

**Outcome 1 Requirements:**
- Describe HR management approaches
- Describe motivation approaches (Maslow, Herzberg, McGregor)
- Explain employee relations impact
- Describe employment legislation impact

**Outcome 2 Requirements:**
- Describe sources of finance with reasons
- Describe purpose of financial statements
- Describe accounting ratios and limitations
