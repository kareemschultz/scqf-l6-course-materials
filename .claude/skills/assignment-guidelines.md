## WRITING TO SOUND HUMAN (NOT AI)

### Words/Phrases to AVOID (AI red flags)
- "Furthermore", "Moreover", "Additionally" (overused)
- "It is important to note that..."
- "In conclusion" at start of every concluding paragraph
- "Utilize" (just say "use")
- "In today's modern world..."
- "This essay will explore..."
- Perfect, robotic sentence structures
- Every paragraph being exactly the same length

### How to Sound Natural
- **Vary sentence length** - Mix short punchy sentences with longer ones
- **Use contractions occasionally** - "don't", "can't", "it's" (sparingly in academic work)
- **Include personal observations** - "I noticed that...", "In my view..."
- **Show your thinking** - "At first, I thought X, but after researching..."
- **Use specific examples** - Not just "a company" but "Tata Motors"
- **Make minor imperfections** - Real students don't write perfectly
- **Read it aloud** - If it sounds robotic, rewrite it

### Sentence Starters to Use Instead
- "Looking at..."
- "One thing that stands out is..."
- "What's interesting here is..."
- "This shows that..."
- "For example..."
- "A good illustration of this is..."
- "In practice, this means..."

## HARVARD REFERENCING

### In-Text Citations
- Single author: (Smith, 2024)
- Two authors: (Smith and Jones, 2024)
- Three+ authors: (Smith et al., 2024)
- Direct quote: (Smith, 2024, p.45)
- No author: (Organisation Name, 2024)
- Website: (Website Name, 2024)

### Reference List Format

**Book:**
Smith, J. (2024) Title of Book in Italics. 2nd edn. Place: Publisher.

**Website:**
Organisation Name (2024) 'Page Title', Website Name. Available at: https://example.com (Accessed: 20 January 2026).

**Journal:**
Smith, J. (2024) 'Article Title', Journal Name, 15(2), pp.45-60.

**Company Report:**
Tata Motors (2024) Annual Report 2024. Mumbai: Tata Motors Limited.

## ACADEMIC INTEGRITY

### DO:
- Use your own words to explain concepts
- Cite ALL sources - books, websites, lectures
- Put direct quotes in quotation marks with page numbers
- Include a full reference list
- Acknowledge help from classmates (if allowed)

### DON'T:
- Copy-paste from websites without citation
- Submit work that isn't yours
- Share your assignment with others
- Use AI to write entire sections without reworking
- Forget to sign the declaration
