## Assignment Submission Requirements

### Mandatory Formatting
- **Font:** Arial or Times New Roman
- **Line Spacing:** 1.5 lines
- **Alignment:** Left-justified paragraphs
- **Footer:** `StudentUSN_Name_UnitCode_UnitTitle_PageNumber` on every page
- **References:** Harvard referencing system

### Required Sections
1. Cover page (use provided template)
2. Statement of Originality and Student Declaration (SIGNED)
3. Main content addressing all learning outcomes
4. References/Bibliography

### Submission Rules

## Unit Learning Outcomes Quick Reference

### J229 76 - Understanding Business

**Outcome 1:** Analyse features, objectives and internal structures of large organisations
- 1.1 Compare features from different sectors (private, public, third)
- 1.2 Identify objectives and describe their importance
- 1.3 Describe internal structures with justification (tall, flat, matrix, etc.)

**Outcome 2:** Analyse the environment in which large organisations operate
- 2.1 Explain impact of internal factors (staffing, finance, technology, culture)
- 2.2 Explain impact of external factors (PESTLE/PESTEC)
- 2.3 Describe stakeholder conflicts of interest

### J22A 76 - Management of People and Finance

**Outcome 1:** Management of people to meet objectives
- 1.1 Describe HR management approaches (recruitment, training, development)
- 1.2 Describe motivation approaches (Maslow's 5 levels with examples)
- 1.3 Explain employee relations impact (5 types of industrial action)
- 1.4 Describe employment legislation impact (H&S, Equality, Data Protection)

**Outcome 2:** Management of finance for effectiveness
- 2.1 Describe 3 finance sources with reasons
- 2.2 Describe 5 purposes of financial statements
- 2.3 Describe 5 accounting ratios and limitations

### HE9E 46 - Contemporary Business Issues

**Outcome 1:** Features and characteristics of SMEs
- 1.1 Define an SME (EU/OECD definition)
- 1.2 Explain 3 key features
- 1.3 Explain characteristics at each of 5 life cycle stages
- 1.4 Outline 2+ objectives for each stage

**Outcome 2:** Analyse SME business strategies
- 2.1 Explore 2 internal + 2 external challenges
- 2.2 Explain role of 4 functions across life cycle
- 2.3 Analyse 2 strategies with expected effects

## Writing Guidelines

### Academic Integrity
- Use Harvard referencing for ALL sources
- Include in-text citations
- Paraphrase effectively - don't just copy
- Declare all collaborative work
- Never share or copy assignments

### Writing Quality
- Be clear and structured
- Use appropriate business terminology
- Include real-world examples
- Address all assessment criteria
- Check spelling and grammar
- Stay within word limits

### To Sound Natural (Not AI-Generated)
- Vary sentence length and structure
- Use first person for reflective tasks
- Include specific examples you've researched
- Show your thinking process
- Avoid overusing words like "furthermore", "moreover", "utilize"
- Read your work aloud - if it sounds robotic, rewrite it
