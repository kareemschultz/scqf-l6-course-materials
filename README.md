## Assignments

### J229 76 — Understanding Business

> Analyse features, objectives, and internal structures of large organisations and their operating environment.

| Task | Requirement | Weight | Companies |
|------|-------------|--------|-----------|
| A1 | Compare features of 3 large orgs from different sectors | 10 marks | Tata Motors, NHS Scotland, Oxfam |
| A2 | Compare organisational types and growth methods | 10 marks | Same 3 organisations |
| A3 | Identify objectives for 4 large orgs | 15 marks | Reliance Jio, Tata Group, Amul, Unilever |
| A4 | Describe 3 internal structures with justification | 15 marks | — |
| B1 | Explain impact of 2 internal factors | 15 marks | Toyota (+), Boeing (−) |
| B2 | PESTEC analysis — 2 external factors | 20 marks | Netflix, McDonald's |
| B3 | Describe 2 stakeholder conflicts of interest | 15 marks | — |

**Split:** Task A 50% · Task B 50%

---

### J22A 76 — Management of People and Finance

> Evaluate how organisations manage people and finance to meet objectives.

| Task | Requirement | Weight |
|------|-------------|--------|
| A1 | 3 HRM approaches (recruitment, training, development) | 15 marks |
| A2 | Maslow's Hierarchy — all 5 levels with workplace examples | 15 marks |
| A3 | 5 types of industrial action + organisational impact | 15 marks |
| A4 | Employment legislation (H&S, Equality, Data Protection) | 15 marks |
| B1 | 3 sources of finance with justification | 10 marks |
| B2 | 5 purposes of financial statements | 15 marks |
| B3 | 5 accounting ratios + limitations | 15 marks |

**Split:** Task A 60% · Task B 40%

---

### HE9E 46 — Contemporary Business Issues

> Analyse features of SMEs and evaluate business strategies across the life cycle.

| Task | Requirement | Weight |
|------|-------------|--------|
| A1 | Define an SME (EU/OECD criteria) | 5 marks |
| A2 | Explain 3 key features of SMEs | 15 marks |
| A3 | Characteristics at each of 5 life cycle stages | 15 marks |
| A4 | 2+ objectives for each life cycle stage | 15 marks |
| B1 | 2 internal + 2 external challenges facing SMEs | 10 marks |
| B2 | Role of 4 functional activities across life cycle | 20 marks |
| B3 | 2 business strategies with expected effects | 20 marks |

**Split:** Task A 50% · Task B 50%
